Customer Care

Questions

Find answers to common questions below. For anything else, reach us directly — we respond within 24–48 hours on business days.

Standard domestic shipping takes 5–7 business days. Expedited options (2–3 days) are available at checkout. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location. You'll receive tracking via email once your order ships.
