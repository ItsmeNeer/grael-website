01

How to Start a Return

Initiating a return is straightforward. Follow the steps below and our team will guide you through the rest.

01

Contact Us

Email graelclothing@gmail.com with your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and your reason. We'll respond within 1–2 business days with confirmation and instructions.

02

Pack Your Item

Securely repackage the item in its original packaging where possible. Include your order number inside the parcel. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags still attached.

03

Ship It Back

Send the package to the return address provided in our confirmation email. We recommend using a tracked shipping method. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is faulty or incorrectly sent.

04

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return (typically within 2–3 business days), we'll process your refund to the original payment method. You'll receive an email confirmation. Refunds appear within 5–7 business days depending on your bank.

02

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

Accepted

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • Original tags still attached
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • In original or equivalent packaging
  • No visible signs of wear or damage

Not Accepted

  • Worn, washed, or altered items
  • Tags removed
  • Returned after 30 days
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Items damaged by customer misuse

03

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for a different size or colour of the same item, subject to availability. To request an exchange, follow the same process as a return and note in your email which size or variant you'd like instead.

If your preferred exchange option is out of stock, we will issue a full refund to your original payment method instead.

As Season One is a limited run, stock levels are finite. We recommend requesting your exchange as soon as possible to avoid missing out on your preferred size.

04

Faulty or Incorrect Items

If you receive an item that is faulty, damaged in transit, or not what you ordered, we will cover the return shipping cost and issue a full refund or send a replacement at no additional cost to you.

  • Email us at graelclothing@gmail.com within 7 days of receiving the item
  • Include your order number and photos of the fault or incorrect item
  • We'll arrange a prepaid return label and process your replacement or refund promptly

05

Shipping Costs

Return shipping is at the customer's expense, except in cases where the item is faulty or we sent the wrong product. We recommend using a tracked service — we cannot be held responsible for returns lost in transit.

Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

06

Refund Timing

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email confirming whether your refund has been approved. If approved, the refund will be applied to your original payment method within the following timeframes:

  • Credit / debit card — 5–7 business days
  • PayPal — 3–5 business days
  • Store credit — issued immediately upon return approval

If you haven't received your refund after 10 business days, please contact us and we'll investigate with the payment processor.

07

Questions

If you have any questions about our returns policy or need help with a specific order, our team is here to assist.
